/ process

01Application

To initiate your journey with us, we require a complete set of KYC documents along with detailed information about your project. This helps us understand your needs and tailor the best financing solution for you.

02Initial Offer

After reviewing the necessary details, we conduct a preliminary underwriting process to draft an initial offer (Heads of Terms), subject to valuation and legal review.

03Formal Offer

Once the valuation is complete, we issue a formal offer. This includes a breakdown of the gross loan amount and associated fees, ensuring full transparency.

04Legal

Our dedicated solicitors coordinate the legal process, managing conveyancing, securing property charges, and finalizing the loan repayment terms with precision and clarity.

05Completion

After all paperwork is signed and formalized, the funds will be promptly disbursed to your account, empowering you to achieve your goals efficiently.

At Azat Capital, we pride ourselves on providing a seamless and secure process, ensuring your success every step of the way.
